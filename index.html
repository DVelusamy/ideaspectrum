Structured incubation for innovation thinking

Method 02

ICLE Framework

Idea Concept Lens Evolution — Multidisciplinary idea expansion
ICLE helps students add depth to their ideas. It uses 14 conceptual lenses across 5 stages to refract a single idea through multiple domains — from numbers and structure, through living systems and human behaviour, to meaning and cosmic-scale futures. Published white paper (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19024288). Works as a physical card deck.

The ICLE Complexity Scale measures how far an idea has evolved: from Seed (ICLE-1) through Structural, Living, Meaningful, to Cosmicle (ICLE-5). This gives educators a way to assess idea maturity.

Method 03

Graffitive

Structured intellectual trespass — Write over what you know
Graffitive helps students stress-test ideas through productive conflict. Students write directly over each other’s ideas using five Spray Cans. The result is a layered palimpsest — a visible record of how ideas evolve through challenge. Graffitive turns critique into a creative act.

Runs as a 90-minute workshop. Students work on shared canvases with different colours for each layer. Faculty can facilitate using the methodology guide.

Method 04

Socratic AI Companion

MBA Dog Bot — AI that asks questions before giving answers
MBA Dog Bot helps students think more carefully with AI rather than just getting answers from it. It operates in Socratic mode by default, prompting students to clarify assumptions, explore implications, and connect ideas. Faculty control what the AI can reference. Foundational Concepts

Two Ideas That Shape Everything

These principles run through every tool in the IdeaSpectrum ecosystem.
Trademarked Concept
Minimum Viable Soul™
The smallest unit of human ideation
Before a business model, before a pitch deck, before a prototype — there is a fragment of genuine human insight. Minimum Viable Soul™ is the irreducible core of an idea: the part that cannot be generated by AI, copied from a competitor, or manufactured in a brainstorm. It is what you actually believe, what you have actually noticed, what you actually care about. Every tool in IdeaSpectrum is designed to protect and develop this core — not to replace it with something faster or louder.
Trademarked Concept
Angel-in-the-Loop™
The ethical layer in every decision
AI can accelerate ideation, but acceleration without ethics produces faster mistakes. Angel-in-the-Loop™ is the principle that every AI-assisted step in IdeaSpectrum includes an ethical checkpoint — a moment where the student is prompted to consider who benefits, who is harmed, what values are embedded, and what assumptions remain unchallenged. It is not a filter or a restriction. It is a habit of mind: the reflex to ask “should we?” before “can we?” The angel is not the AI. The angel is the student.
